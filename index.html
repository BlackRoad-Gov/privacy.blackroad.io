What We Collect
›
Server Logs
Basic HTTP request logs on Caddy. IP address, URL requested, timestamp, response code. Standard web server logging. Rotated and deleted after 30 days.
›
Stripe Data
When you make a payment, Stripe processes it. We see your email and payment status. We don't see your card number. Stripe's privacy policy applies.
›
That's It
No cookies (except essential session cookies if you log in). No analytics scripts. No Google Analytics. No Facebook Pixel. No Hotjar. No Mixpanel. Nothing.
What We Don't
›
No Behavioral Tracking
We don't track what you click, how long you stay, what you read, or where you go next. We don't build profiles.
›
No Data Sales
We will never sell, share, or trade your data. Not to advertisers. Not to data brokers. Not to AI training companies.
›
No AI Training on Your Data
If you use our AI features, your prompts stay on our fleet. They're not sent to OpenAI, Anthropic, or any cloud provider. They're not used for model training.
›
No Third-Party Scripts
No external JavaScript loaded except Google Fonts. No analytics, no ads, no social media trackers, no CDN-hosted libraries that phone home.
›
Your Right to Delete
Email alexa@blackroad.io. We'll delete everything we have on you. There isn't much to delete, but the right is absolute.
